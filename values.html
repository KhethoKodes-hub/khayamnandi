Our CSR Approach

Khaya-Mnandi Supply and Catering supports various initiatives which are aimed at improving the lives of the people in our local communities, both from a financial and social perspective.

We believe that business success should translate to community development, which is why we invest a portion of our profits back into programs that uplift those around us.

Our Journey

2012

Founded by Monica Nappie with a vision to provide quality catering services to the mining sector.

2015

Expanded services to include supply chain solutions and launched first CSR initiative.

2018

Awarded first major mining contract and established school feeding program.

2021

Recognized as a leading BWO enterprise in the Northern Cape province.

2023

Awarded two catering service contracts at King and Bruce Mine in Kathu.
