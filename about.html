About Our Company

Committed to excellence since 2012

OUR STORY

Building Excellence in Catering & Supply

Khaya-Mnandi Supply and Catering was founded in 2012 by entrepreneur Monica Nappie. Based in Postmasburg, the company is ideally located in the Tsantsabane Region of Siyanda District in the Northern Cape Province.

Monica has extensive experience in the catering industry, having initially worked for Royal Minandi with the responsibility of managing significant catering contracts in the mining sector.

100% Black Women Owned 100% Black Female Management

Our Vision

To create a service-based company whose goal is to exceed clients' expectations and to increase our client base through superior performance.

Our Mission

To be the leading general supplier and preferred caterer in the Northern Cape Province, growing our business at a rate that is profitable and manageable.

Our Growth

Supported by a burgeoning mining industry and growth in the local economy of Postmasburg and Kathu, we continue to expand our services.
